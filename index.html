Policy as a Catalyst: Regulatory Timeline

The government has systematically built a regulatory framework that transforms sustainability from a voluntary exercise into a mandatory business function.

2017: The Foundation

OJK Regulation No. 51 mandates sustainability reporting for financial institutions and public companies.

2021: The Mechanism

Presidential Regulation No. 98 establishes the legal framework for a carbon economic value.

2023: The Marketplace

The Indonesia Carbon Exchange (IDXCarbon) officially launches, operationalizing the carbon market.

2027: The Future

Planned adoption of IFRS S1 & S2 standards will elevate reporting rigor to global best-practice levels.
